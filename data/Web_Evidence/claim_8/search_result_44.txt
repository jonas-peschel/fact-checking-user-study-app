Joe Biden
@JoeBidenJoseph Robinette Biden Jr. is an American politician who was the 46th president of the United States from 2021 to 2025. A member of the Democratic Party, he served as the 47th vice president from 2009 to 2017 under the 44th President Barack Obama and also represented Delaware in the U.S. Senate from 1973 to 2009.
