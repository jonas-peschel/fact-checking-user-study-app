NEW DELHI: Consortium of National Law Universities on Friday decided to reschedule the CLAT 2020 examination to September 28. Earlier, the exam was scheduled to be held on September 7 which has now been postponed to September 28.
The CLAT 2020 examination for both UG and PG candidates will be held on September 28.
Issuing a notice on Friday, Convenor, CLAT 2020 Professor Balraj Chauhan said that the CLAT 2020 examination for both UG and PG candidates scheduled for 7th September, 2020 has been postponed to Monday, the 28th September, 2020 from 2 to 4 PM.
The Executive Committee of the Consortium of National Law Universities held a meeting on August 27, to review the precautionary measures taken in the context of the COVID 19 pandemic and the resulting situation in various states across the country.
The Executive Committee had assessed the prevailing lockdown situation in various parts of the country and in particular, took into consideration the lockdown announced in the state of West Bengal for September 7, and the continued lockdown in the state of Bihar until the September 6. On this basis, the Consortium has decided to postpone the CLAT examination.
