CLAT 2020 to be held on September 7, check important details here
CLAT 2020 exam is scheduled to be held on September 7, 2020. Admit cards will be issued from August 24, 2020.
Attention candidates, the Consortium of National Law Universities has announced a new date for CLAT 2020. As per information, the eligibility test of CLAT 2020 is scheduled for September 7, 2020. It will be held between 2 pm to 4 pm. The decision to conduct CLAT 2020 on September 7, 2020, has been taken at the meeting of the General Body of Consortium of NLUs, which was held on August 10, 2020.
The meeting was held “to review the precautionary measures taken in the context of Covid-19 pandemic and settle on a feasible date for conducting the CLAT 2020 exam”.
CLAT 2020: Important information
CLAT 2020 will be held online mode at designated centres across India. Students can download the admit cards for CLAT 2020 from August 24 by visiting the official website i.e. consortiumofnlus.ac.in. Earlier, CLAT 2020 exam was scheduled for August 22, 2020.
“The Consortium is in the process of securing further permissions so that the Admit Cards of the candidates shall be considered as Movement Passes by local authorities to allow candidates to move between Containment zones and travel to their designated examination centres,” as per a statement issued in this regard.
Candidates are requested to keep an eye on the official website for any further updates. The consortium will review centre locations on an ongoing basis to ensure the safety of students and staff.
