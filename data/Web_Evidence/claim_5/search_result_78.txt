About The Common Law Admission Test
The document summarizes information about the Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) which is an entrance exam for admissions to undergraduate and postgraduate law programs at 11 National Law Universities in India. It provides details about the participating universities, eligibility criteria for the exam, pattern of questions, and admission process.
